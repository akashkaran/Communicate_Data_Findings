Investigation Overview¶

This dataset contains data from Bike sharing service Bay Wheels fromerly known as Ford Go Bike. This visualization primarily focuses on finding the trends and patterns in trips and customer in a year of 2019.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset is from official Bay Wheels site by month and then combined for yearly analysis. The dataset has total of 2506983 records and 15 features with information of user types and information about there trips.

Relationship between day of the week and type of user for trips¶

There is drop in count for subscribers on Saturday and Sunday using the bikes. So our graph asuggest that there is a possibility that most of the subscribers are using rides as their commute to work.
